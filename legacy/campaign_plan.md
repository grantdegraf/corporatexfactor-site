## PHASE 1 — Campaign Identity

**Project Name:** Legacy Project  
**Campaign Name:** Voices of Courage  
**Tagline:** *"Preserving the stories that must never be forgotten."*

**Mission Statement:**
> The Legacy Project is a cultural and educational initiative preserving stories of courage during the Holocaust and confronting modern antisemitism through documentary storytelling, educational content, and media production.

**Two Framing Tracks:**
- **Track A — Holocaust Funders** (Claims Conference, Covenant, Yad Vashem USA, MSHEF): Lead with intergenerational Holocaust connection, survivor testimony, and historical rigor.
- **Track B — Civic/Antisemitism Funders** (Natan Fund, JFNA Antisemitism grants, ADL, community federations): Lead with post-Oct 7 civic resilience, campus education, and confronting contemporary antisemitism.

**Donor Deliverables:**
- Documentary storytelling projects featuring survivor narratives
- Educational curriculum for campus and K-12 programs
- Public awareness media campaigns
- Digital distribution of completed content
- Community screening events
