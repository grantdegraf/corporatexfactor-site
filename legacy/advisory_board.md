## Advisory Board Invitation Letter

**Subject:** Invitation to Join the Legacy Project Advisory Board — Voices of Courage

Dear [Name],

I am writing to extend a personal invitation to join the Advisory Board of the Legacy Project, a cultural and educational initiative dedicated to preserving Holocaust memory and confronting modern antisemitism through documentary storytelling and media production.

**About the Initiative**

The Legacy Project's "Voices of Courage" campaign is producing documentary films, educational content, and public awareness media that captures firsthand survivor testimony and transmits it to new generations. Produced from Jerusalem by Sipur Studios, our work is designed for campus education, community engagement, and global digital distribution.

With antisemitism rising to its highest recorded levels since World War II, and with Holocaust survivors in their final years, the urgency of this work has never been greater.

**The Advisory Board Role**

We are assembling a small, distinguished Advisory Board of professionals who share our conviction that these stories must be told. The role is:

- **Time commitment**: 2–4 hours per year (one advisory call + availability for introductions)
- **No operational requirement**: We ask only for your name, your counsel when sought, and occasional introductions to donors or institutional partners where appropriate
- **Reputational context**: Your association lends credibility to our work in grant applications, donor conversations, and institutional partnerships
- **Impact**: Your guidance helps shape productions that will educate hundreds of thousands of students and community members for decades

**Why Now**

We are at a pivotal moment. The window to capture survivor testimony is closing. The need for credible, rigorous Holocaust education content has never been more acute. And the philanthropic landscape is responding — with Claims Conference, Covenant Foundation, and major Jewish family offices actively seeking partners doing serious work.

Your involvement at this founding stage would mean a great deal — both to the integrity of the project and to its trajectory.

I would welcome a brief call to share more about the project and answer any questions you may have.

With deep respect for your work and your time,

Grant de Graf  
Producer, Legacy Project / Sipur Studios  
Jerusalem, Israel  
grantdegraf@gmail.com
