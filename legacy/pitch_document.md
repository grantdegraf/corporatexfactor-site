**The Legacy Project | Sipur Studios | 2026**

## The Story We Must Tell

Antisemitism is at its highest recorded level since World War II. Holocaust survivors are passing. The generation who can say "I was there" is nearly gone.

The Legacy Project exists to ensure their stories are never lost — and that the lessons of the Holocaust remain alive in classrooms, communities, and cultural memory for generations to come.

---

## What We Do

The Legacy Project produces documentary storytelling, educational content, and public awareness media that:

- **Preserves firsthand survivor testimony** before it is lost forever
- **Educates new generations** through curriculum-integrated film and digital content
- **Confronts modern antisemitism** with historical clarity and moral urgency
- **Amplifies Jewish resilience** stories on a global media platform

Our productions feature the intergenerational connections between Holocaust survivors and their descendants — stories of courage, resistance, and survival that resonate across communities worldwide.

## The Opportunity

Post-October 7, the urgency of Holocaust education and antisemitism confrontation has reached a new threshold. Funders, institutions, and communities are actively seeking credible, impactful media partners to carry this work forward.
