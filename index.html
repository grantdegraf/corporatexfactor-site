What We Do

CorporateXFactor provides structured advisory services for organizations and principals managing judgment assets, pursuing recovery positioning, or evaluating monetization pathways for legal awards and claims. Engagements are conducted on a confidential basis. Our approach is institutional, disciplined, and oriented toward realistic outcome frameworks — not speculative positioning.

We work with clients who require a structured, objective framework for evaluating and advancing recovery pathways. We do not accept mandates where the pathway is not viable. We do not inflate expectations.

  • Judgment monetization advisory
  • Recovery positioning strategy
  • Asset enforcement pathway analysis
  • Stakeholder alignment and structure

How It Works

  1. Initial Assessment — We evaluate the judgment or claim, enforcement landscape, and counterparty position to establish a realistic advisory framework. Viability is assessed before any engagement is accepted.
  2. Strategy Development — We develop a structured positioning strategy aligned with client objectives, risk tolerance, and timeline requirements. Strategy is grounded in enforceability, not aspiration.
  3. Execution Support — We support implementation, stakeholder engagement, and ongoing strategic adjustment throughout the recovery process.

Engagements are scoped, confidential, and structured around your mandate. We do not accept retainers for non-viable pathways.

Risk Discipline

Judgment recovery is a structured discipline, not a speculative exercise. CorporateXFactor applies a controlled, institutional approach to every engagement — beginning with an honest assessment of enforceability, counterparty capacity, and jurisdictional constraints.

We do not pursue engagements where the recovery pathway is not viable. We do not overstate outcomes. Our value is in rigorous, objective advisory that prepares clients for realistic results. The discipline of knowing when not to proceed is as important as knowing how to advance.
