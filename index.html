What We Do

CorporateXFactor provides structured advisory services for organizations and principals managing judgment assets, pursuing recovery positioning, or evaluating monetization pathways for legal awards and claims.

Engagements are conducted on a confidential basis. Our approach is institutional, disciplined, and oriented toward realistic outcome frameworks.

  • Judgment monetization advisory
  • Recovery positioning strategy
  • Asset enforcement pathway analysis
  • Stakeholder alignment and structure

How It Works

  1. I
    Initial Assessment

    We evaluate the judgment or claim, enforcement landscape, and relevant counterparty position to establish a realistic advisory framework.

  2. II
    Strategy Development

    We develop a structured positioning strategy aligned with the client's objectives, risk tolerance, and timeline requirements.

  3. III
    Execution Support

    We support implementation, stakeholder engagement, and ongoing strategic adjustment throughout the recovery process.

Risk Discipline

Judgment recovery is a structured discipline, not a speculative exercise. CorporateXFactor applies a controlled, institutional approach to every engagement — beginning with an honest assessment of enforceability, counterparty capacity, and jurisdictional constraints.

We do not pursue engagements where the recovery pathway is not viable. We do not overstate outcomes. Our value is in rigorous, objective advisory that prepares clients for realistic results.
