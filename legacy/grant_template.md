## SECTION 2: PROJECT TITLE & SUMMARY

**Project Title:** Voices of Courage — Holocaust Storytelling and Education Initiative

**One-Paragraph Summary (Track A — Holocaust Funders):**
The Legacy Project's "Voices of Courage" initiative produces documentary films and educational content preserving firsthand Holocaust survivor testimony and transmitting it to new generations. Grounded in historical rigor, our productions feature intergenerational narrative arcs — the living connections between survivors and their descendants — designed for university and K-12 curriculum integration, campus programming, and digital distribution. With survivors now in their final years, this project addresses an urgent and irreversible preservation challenge.

**One-Paragraph Summary (Track B — Antisemitism / Civic Funders):**
The Legacy Project's "Voices of Courage" initiative produces documentary storytelling content that connects Holocaust history to contemporary antisemitism — equipping students, communities, and educators with the historical context and moral clarity to confront rising hatred. Operating from Jerusalem in the aftermath of October 7, our productions are designed for campus education programs, community screenings, and global digital distribution, with a focus on building resilience through memory and historical understanding.

---

## SECTION 3: PROBLEM STATEMENT

Antisemitism is at its highest recorded level in post-WWII history. Holocaust survivors are in their final years — the living generation who can testify "I was there" is nearly gone. Without active intervention, firsthand testimony will be lost, and Holocaust education will be reduced to textbooks and artifacts. Simultaneously, a new generation of students — many of whom have no family connection to the Holocaust — is encountering antisemitism on campuses and in public life with inadequate historical grounding to understand its origins and dangers.
